Sustainable Agriculture

Sustainable agriculture is a practice that aims to ensure the availability of food and other resources for future generations. It encompasses many different practices and approaches, all of which are important. One way we work to support sustainable agriculture is by making sure that there is always availability without depletion. We do this by making sure that we only use what we need, and then moving it to where it's needed next.
